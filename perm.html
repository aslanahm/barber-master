Piggyback Perming

A piggyback perm application involves applying two different perm solutions in succession to achieve customized curl patterns or textures in the hair.

BrickLayer Perming

A bricklayer perm is a perm technique where the rods are placed in a brick-like pattern, alternating the placement of the rods in each row. This creates a staggered effect similar to the layout of bricks in a wall. The result is a natural-looking perm with varied curl sizes and distribution throughout the hair, providing texture and volume.

Straightback Perming

Straightback perming is a technique that curls the hair predominantly towards the back while leaving the front sections straight or slightly waved, resulting in a sleek and controlled look with defined curls
